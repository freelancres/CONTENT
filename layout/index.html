Dortmund

The once important imperial and Hanseatic city ( Tremonia in Latin ) along the Hellweg , which presumably goes back to the founding of a Carolingian imperial court , is now developing from an industrial metropolis into an important service and technology location: formerly known primarily for steel , coal and beer.
